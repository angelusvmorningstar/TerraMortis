# Story 2.3: Professional Training Grant System

**Status:** Draft

## Story

**As a** Storyteller,
**I want** the system to automatically apply Professional Training asset skills and dot-level benefit grants from a character's PT merit `role` field,
**so that** PT benefits appear accurately on character sheets and feed correctly into skill pools and influence totals.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. For each character with a Professional Training merit, the system reads the `role` field and applies `nine_again: true` to the 2 asset skills defined for that role at render time (without mutating stored data).
2. Asset skill bonus dots from PT (if any are granted at given PT levels) appear in skill pool calculations via `attrTotal` / `skTotal` without being stored permanently on the character.
3. PT dot-level benefit merits (e.g. Contacts, Allies at specific ratings) are injected as `derived: true` merits at render time, at the correct dot level for the character's PT rating.
4. PT-derived merits appear on the sheet in the appropriate merit section and are included in influence totals.
5. Removing or downgrading a PT merit (edit mode) removes all its derived grants on the next render.
6. The PT role-to-skills mapping is stored in `data/merits_db.json` (or a dedicated `data/pt_roles.json`) -- not hardcoded in JS.
7. Viktor Ashwood (PT4) and Marcus Webb (PT4) render correctly with no console errors.

### Nine-Again Rule
- `nine_again: true` on a skill means that skill grants 9-again by default, e.g. via Professional Training [ref: data-models.md, Skill section]
- This is applied at render time, not stored on the character -- do not mutate `char.skills[s].nine_again` directly for PT-granted bonus
