What is Terra Mortis

A game of undead politics and personal horror.

Monthly parlour LARP set in the modern nights of Sydney, using Vampire: The Requiem 2nd Edition by Onyx Path Publishing.

The Game

Parlour LARP, Monthly

Terra Mortis runs monthly in Sydney's inner west for 30–40 players. Sessions are approximately four hours. No costuming requirement — we focus on character, story, and political manoeuvre.

The Setting

Modern Nights, Sydney

You are Kindred — vampires navigating the treacherous politics of Sydney's undead court. Ancient grudges, fragile alliances, and the constant threat of breach and Final Death define every session.

The Experience

Character-Driven Arcs

Every character has a history, ambitions, and a Beast slowly eroding their humanity. Progress is earned through downtime actions, social manoeuvring, and the choices made when the stakes are highest.

Player Area

Access your character between sessions.

The Player Portal is available to all active Terra Mortis players. Sign in with Discord to pick up where you left off.

  • Character sheet — stats, Disciplines, merits, and XP tracking
  • Downtime submissions — choose your actions each cycle before the deadline
  • Ordeal progress — track your personal story goals across the chronicle
  • Cycle outcomes — read published results once the ST team processes the cycle
