What is Terra Mortis

A game of undead politics and personal horror.

Monthly parlour LARP set in the modern nights of Sydney, using Vampire: The Requiem 2nd Edition by Onyx Path Publishing.

The Game

Parlour LARP, Monthly

Terra Mortis runs monthly in Sydney's inner west for 10–20 players. Sessions are approximately four hours. No costuming requirement — we focus on character, story, and political manoeuvre.

The Setting

Modern Nights, Sydney

You are Kindred — vampires navigating the treacherous politics of Sydney's undead court. Ancient grudges, fragile alliances, and the constant threat of breach and Final Death define every session.

The Experience

Character-Driven Arcs

Every character has a history, ambitions, and a Beast slowly eroding their humanity. Progress is earned through downtime actions, social manoeuvring, and the choices made when the stakes are highest.

New Players

How to join the domain.

Terra Mortis welcomes players with some experience of tabletop or live-action roleplay. Here is how to get started.

01
Read the Primer

Download the new player guide to understand the setting, the rules framework, and what to expect at your first session. No book purchase required — the primer covers everything you need.

02
Build a Character

Work with the ST team before your first session. We will help you create a Kindred that fits the chronicle and has room to grow. Our character app keeps your sheet accessible between sessions.

03
Come to Game

Confirm your attendance before game night, show up, and step into the domain. Sessions begin promptly. The community is welcoming to new players and experienced LARP veterans alike.

Safety and Consent Terra Mortis uses Lines and Veils, the X-card, and pre-game check-ins. Themes include violence, manipulation, and moral failure in a horror context. Players define their own limits and these are respected at all times. Content concerns can be raised with the ST team at any time.
