# Terra Mortis — Merits Errata

*House rules and errata for Terra Mortis LARP. Where errata conflicts with book text, errata wins.*

---

## Cashing In Merits

With Storyteller approval, a player may voluntarily trade in a Merit for Experiences, provided it is not done to exploit temporary benefits. If a Merit has run its course and no longer makes sense for your character, you may use those points elsewhere.

---

## Permanent Merits

Some Merits, such as Ambidextrous, Eidetic Memory, and Fighting Styles, represent inherent abilities and cannot be exchanged unless the character undergoes a major change, such as losing a limb.
